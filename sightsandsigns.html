Sights and Signage

A collection of images and signs that made my day (and may make yours too)

Albania Exclusives


I quickly snapped a photo of the George W. Bush bakery as we drove by. Bush Jr. is to Albania what Bill Clinton is to Kosovo - there's another bakery named after him in a different part of town, along with a "George W Bush Road" downtown.

Berlin has der Ampelman, Tirana has the national mascot (a two-headed eagle) to guide foot traffic.

A painting reflecting citizen's reactions to learning that Skanderberg, Albania's national hero, had died. The kid in the bottom-left corner is giving legendary side-eye.

This edited stop sign was so ubiquitious I almost thought it was authorized by the city.
